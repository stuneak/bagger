01

What the Extension Does

The Sopeko Chrome extension displays stock pick performance badges next to Reddit usernames. When you browse Reddit, the extension fetches publicly available stock pick data from the Sopeko API.

  • Displays performance badges next to Reddit usernames
  • Fetches publicly available stock pick data from Sopeko servers
  • Works only on reddit.com domains
02

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be crystal clear: the Sopeko extension does not collect any information about you or your browsing habits.

  • No personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • No browsing history or activity tracking
  • No Reddit account information or credentials
  • No cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels
  • No data sold or shared with third parties
03

Permissions Explained

The extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Host permission (reddit.com)
    Required to read Reddit usernames on the page and display badges.
  • Network access (sopeko.com)
    Required to fetch stock pick performance data from our API.
04

Open Source

The Sopeko Chrome extension is open source. You can review the complete source code to verify our privacy claims. We believe in transparency and welcome community scrutiny.

05

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page and notify users through the Chrome Web Store update notes. Any changes that affect data collection will require explicit approval.

06
